<slicc-pill>

A standalone custom element: a label pill with a cone/scoop icon. The icon has the rolling-eye effect, parametrizable color, and an eyes state (open / none / dead). The pill itself is the clip path — by default zoomed in on the eyes; hover (or focus) zooms out to the full icon. The label rides above the icon, and the component adapts to light/dark (auto via prefers-color-scheme, or forced with theme="light|dark").

Eye tuning


        
      

      
Drag the sliders to reposition the eyes live across every pill. The zoom auto-centers on the eye box.
