Here’s the full audit. The current hero leads with a near-black canvas and a monospaced headline, which signals "developer tool" more than "product". The button row offers six equally-weighted actions, so nothing reads as the primary path, and the small mono body copy slips below a comfortable contrast ratio. My recommendation: a warm paper canvas, one large display headline, and a single high-contrast CTA — leaving the rest of the tokens intact so the change stays low-risk.
